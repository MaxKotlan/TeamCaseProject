Saturday Tutoring

Serve Compassionately


The Saturday Tutoring Program (STP), in its seventh year, brings together caring adults to help students succeed. The free tutoring program helps students in Grades 1 - 6 from school districts in the Hillcrest area.


Saturday Tutoring is from 10AM to 12 Noon in Bloomfield Hall (back building)
